About Victorious Festival 2022

Victorious Festival has taken the festival scene by storm. Known for its incredibly affordable price tag, stunning seaside location and giant FREE kids arena it's become a firm favourite for families in the South.

Victorious Festival will return to Southsea Common for the 2022 August bank holiday.

Music genres: Rock, Pop, Indie, Alternative

Victorious Festival line up

Multi-award winning Victorious Festival (26/27/28 August 2022)V– the UK’s biggest metropolitan festival – has today announced it’s first wave of artists for next summer.

 

Headlining Friday night is a band that needs no introduction – Stereophonics. Forged in the crucible of Britpop, The Welsh rockers mark an amazing 30 years together in 2022 so expect a huge celebration and with eleven studio albums under their belts and their highly anticipated twelth – Oochya! – launching in spring, it’ll be a must-see set brimming with stone-cold singalong classics and some brand new material to boot.

 

 

Also appearing on Friday are Mancunian indie legends James. Coming into their fifth decade and rightly renowed as one of the best live bands in the business, James never fail to deliver the goods and are perfect for the opening night of Victorious 2022.

 

 

Liverpudlian three-piece The Wombats bring their infectious brand of indie-pop to the party on Saturday while the afternoon belongs to Brit-winning, chart-topping RnB/pop royalty Sugababes with an extra-special daytime show. Sunshine? Check. Cocktails? Check. Dancing shoes? Check? Freak Like Me at full volume? Check check check!!

 

Sunday sees performances from acclaimed alt-rockers Nothing But Theives and the Duchess of Pop herself, award winning nu-disco diva Sophie Ellis-Bextor.

 

 

Elsewhere across the weekend the bill is loaded with top-notch talent including experimental pop from Self Esteem, New York indie trio We Are Scientists, singer-songwriter Becky Hill, Sheffiled rockers Little Man Tate, Baby Queen, Coach Party, Worry Worry and Sam Ryder.

 

 

Speaking about next year’s festival Andy Marsh, festival director, said: ‘‘It’s nice to get excited about Victorious this side of Christmas. We’ve revealed here a little taste of what to expect next year with some massive acts confirmed but there’s so much still to come. 2022 is going to be a vintage year for Victorious. Watch this space!”

 

 

Early bird tickets for Victorious Festival are on sale now from just £40 per day (fees apply)

Offsite Camping / Farlington Fields

No Camping is permitted inside the Event.There is offsite camping available at the Farlington Fields, which are located just off the M27/A27, Portsmouth.

Camping in the Farlington Fields is only permitted if you purchase a Ticket to the Event. Anyone found camping without an appropriate Ticket and/or Wristband will be removed. One camping ticket is needed per person.

You must buy the appropriate camping ticket for your age. You must be aged 18 or over to purchase camping tickets (including tickets for those under 18 years of age)

If you are age 18 or over and are buying a ticket for someone under the age of 18  you must either be the parent, legal guardian or nominated guardian responsible for young person or child who you are purchasing the camping ticket for. You must complete the Legal/Responsible Adult Guardian form and bring it with you to the Farlington Fields in order to gain entry.

All people under the age of 18 must be accompanied by an adult. If you are accompanying people aged 13 – 17 you must be 26 or over. A maximum of 4 persons aged 13 – 17 will be allowed entry to the Farlington Fields with each adult aged 26 or over.

Weekend Camping tickets include entry to all 3 days of the event.

Camping tickets should be purchased in advance from our or Official Ticketing Agent’s website.

The price of your camping ticket is fully guaranteed at the time that it is booked (subject only to the correction of errors). We reserve the right to correct errors in both advertised and confirmed price.

Camping tickets are non-transferable and non-refundable.

Camping tickets will be exchanged for wristbands on entry into the Farlington Fields. Wristbands cannot be issued until the day/date as stated on the Ticket is checked and will only be issued directly to the Ticket Holder on production of photographic ID (driver’s licence or passport). It is not possible to collect wristbands on behalf of other people and all wristbands must be placed and secured on the individual’s wrist directly by our staff. Your wristband will be invalidated if any part of it is removed, detached, altered or defaced. Wristbands will not be reissued or replaced regardless of whether you still have your camping ticket.

You must wear your wristbands at all times whilst within the Farlington Fields Offsite Campsite and the event and wristbands must be produced for inspection upon our request and/or the request of any Authorised Person. Failure to do so may result in you being ejected from the Farlington Fields without refund or compensation.

Those with a Young Persons Camping Ticket or Child Camping Ticket will only be able to enter the Farlington Fields with the adult who purchased their ticket (e.g. their parent, legal guardian or nominated guardian). Any young person or child attempting to enter the Farlington Fields unaccompanied will be denied entry and will have to wait at welfare until their parent, legal guardian or nominated guardian collects them. Abuse of this system may result in all parties being asked to leave the Farlington Fields and the Event.

Those with a Young Persons Camping Ticket or Child Camping Ticket must camp with their parent, legal guardian or nominated guardian.

Cars or vans are not allowed into the Farlington Fields and must be parked in the adjacent Parking field. You will need to purchase a campsite car parking ticket in advance if you wish to park here.

Searches will be undertaken upon entry and re-admittance to ensure compliance with the provisions of these Conditions.

You should bring a tent that is sized for the amount of occupants. We reserve the right to refuse to allow any structures which are too large or which we believe may cause a health and safety risk.

We are happy for groups to camp near each other within the zone but we politely ask that people are considerate and do not to set up overly-large “camping areas” to ensure everyone has enough room to enjoy the campsite and all have a fair amount of space.

Anti-social behaviour will not be tolerated and you may be ejected from the Farlington Fields and the Event without refund or compensation. If you are ejected from the Farlington Fields, you will also be refused entry to the Event.

We anticipate that the Farlington Fields will contain toilets, showers, food stalls, a 24 hour security, medical and welfare presence, lighting, cooking areas, rubbish disposal area, mobile phone charging, but we will not be liable for any failure to provide any of the advertised facilities.

We do not accept any responsibility for theft, damage, loss or breakage to any of your property which you choose to bring and/or leave at the campsite. All items brought to the Farlington Fields are brought and left at your own risk.

The list of Prohibited Items in paragraph 29 of the Ticket Conditions shall apply save that, kitchen knives with a blade length of less than 3 inches (e.g. table knife or small paring knife) and gas canisters (under 5kg) are allowed for use in the cooking area of the campsite.

You are not permitted to bring personal portable toilets into the Farlington Fields due to sanitary and hygiene considerations.

You are permitted to bring your own pre-cooked food and soft drinks for your own consumption in the campsites but not for resale. In addition, you are only permitted to bring either 24 cans of beer/lager and/or cider per person OR 2 bottles of wine OR 1 bottle of spirit (which must all be decanted into plastic bottles) per person into the campsites. Glass and spirits and alcoholic beverages with alcohol content which exceed 40% ABV are strictly prohibited. Searches will be undertaken upon entry and re-admittance to ensure compliance with the provisions of these Conditions. Any alcohol above the permitted allowance will be confiscated. No food or drink can be taken from the Farlington Fields into the Shuttle Bus or the Event.

You will indemnify us against the full value of any loss damage or excessive soling to the Farlington Fields howsoever caused, unless caused by us or an Authorised Person.

We will not be responsible for any claims for the injury to persons or loss or damage to property howsoever caused unless such injury or damage was caused by faulty material or workmanship or negligence on our part or that of an Authorised Person.

We are not responsible for your travel to and from the Farlington Fields or for any expenses including travel, accommodation, subsistence or loss of earnings caused by delays howsoever caused.

The Farlington Fields Offsite Campsite will open at 10.00am on the Friday; at 08.00am on the Saturday and at 8.00am on the Sunday of the Event. The Farlington Fields will close at 01.00am on Friday, Saturday and Sunday morning, so make sure you are back on time or you will be turned away. You must leave the Farlington Fields by 12.00 on the Monday immediately following the Event at the latest.

Offsite Camping – Campervan Zone / Farlington Fields

All standard camping terms and conditions are applicable in addition to the live-in vehicles specific terms outlined in this section.

Entry to the Campervan Zone will only be granted if your vehicle:

is a purpose-built vehicle with camping facilities (i.e. not a van or car with a makeshift temporary mattress placed within it);

has a waste water storage tank or container on or in the vehicle; and

has a permanently fixed means of storage, a cupboard, locker or wardrobe.

Entry to the Campervan Zone is only possible with the advance purchase of a Campervan Pitch ticket (as well as an event ticket). One Campervan Pitch ticket is required per campervan / similar live-in vehicle.

The Campervan Pitch ticket will not grant the holder entry to the Event. The holder of a Campervan Pitch ticket will be denied entry to the Venue if they are not in possession of an event Ticket and/or Wristband.

Campervan Pitch tickets should be purchased in advance from our or Official Ticketing Agent’s website.

Campervan Pitch tickets are non-transferable and non-refundable.

We reserve the right to search all vehicles upon entry.

Campervan or similar live-in vehicles (including their contents) are brought and left at your own risk.

Campervan or similar live-in vehicles should not be parked in the fire lanes.

No re-admission is permitted. If you remove your campervan or similar live-in vehicle from the field, you will not be able to re-enter.

We do not accept any responsibility for theft, damage, loss or breakage to your campervan or similar live-in vehicles (or its contents).

For the purpose of mobile vehicle pass holders in the mobile vehicle field only, gas canisters which have been professionally installed with certification and BBQ (as long as they’re used in the designated cooking areas) shall be removed from the list of Prohibited Items in paragraph 29(b) of the Ticket Conditions.

No open air fires are allowed.

Cars or vans are allowed into Campervan Zone if towing a mobile vehicle but then they must be removed as cars or vans must not be parked in the campervan/caravan area at any time.

Room for Campervans or similar live-in vehicles is limited. You will be allowed 7 x 8 meters of space maximum.

It is your responsibility to ensure all vehicles and other belongings fit into this area – tents and awnings are allowed as long as they fit within your area. Items found outside of your area by us or an Authorised Person are liable to be removed with no compensation payable.

Pitches will be allocated on a first come, first served basis and if you want to park next to friends, you will need to arrive together.

Only one campervan or similar live-in vehicle is allowed per pitch.

Personal photographic identification will be requested upon entry into the mobile vehicle field.

Electricity and water hook ups are not available as standard. You can purchase a power connection when you buy your Campervan Pitch Ticket – 10amps MAX, with only PAT tested equipment. If you draw or attempt to draw more power than this or are found using non pat tested equipment your supply will be cut and no refund will be given.

All water water and toilet waste must be disposed of in the containers provided.

No trading is allowed anywhere within the motor vehicle fields.

The Shuttle Bus service is free for Ticket Holders.

You can make an unlimited number of journeys back and forth provided that you have a Ticket to the Event.

The journey takes approximately 30 – 40 minutes one way.

We reserve the right to change running times to meet operational requirements without notification. It is your responsibility to ensure you have given yourself enough time if you have somewhere to be. It is very likely there will be queues at the busier times for the coaches and traffic conditions can be unpredictable.
