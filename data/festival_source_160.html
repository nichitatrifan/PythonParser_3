About Loveland Festival 2022

Loveland turns 25 and it will be a celebration that you will never, ever forget. The beautiful Sloterpark, a line-up of the best house & techno acts, interactive entertainment and many, many surprises.

--

With more than 300 acres, Sloterpark is Amsterdam’s largest park. Located in Amsterdam’s western neighbourhoods (Nieuw-West), it was developed in the 1950s across the lake Sloterplas. There are lovely nature trails through the park, and separate pedestrian trails in spots. The park is the ideal location for a summer festival.

 

Music genres: House, Electronic
